About the designer

Hello and welcome to my webpage. I am a self-proclaimed web designer and developer that will be a recent graduate come January 2021 with a Bachelors degree in Information Technology.

I have always had an interest in web designing and developing since the days of Myspace when building that perfect profile look required you to learn how to code your own HTML. It opened a can of worms for my love of building websites since then.

Most of my experience has been in HTML and CSS when creating websites. I have slightly dabbed in JavaScript and Python3 but I will be diving into those languages as soon as I can. I am able to also build responsive websites and I love using Bootstrap4 to build.

I am always ready for the next challenge that will test my skills and help me learn new things.
